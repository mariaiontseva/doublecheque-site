Complaints

Last updated 2 July 2026

We aim to give you a straightforward, high-quality service. If something falls short, please tell us – we take complaints seriously and want to put things right.

1. How to complain

Please contact us with the details of your complaint, including what happened and what you'd like us to do:

  • Email: farid@doublecheque.com
  • By post: Double Cheque Ltd, 411 Office 1.01, Oxford Street, London W1C 2PE

2. What happens next

We will acknowledge your complaint promptly and look into it fairly. Where your complaint concerns a regulated activity, it will be handled in line with Financial Conduct Authority rules by the responsible authorised firm – Palace Ventures Limited (our FCA principal, FRN 433291) and/or Sapphire Capital Partners LLP (the fund's manager, FRN 565716) – and you will receive a final response, normally within eight weeks.

3. Financial Ombudsman Service

If you are an eligible complainant and you are not satisfied with our final response, or eight weeks have passed, you may be able to refer your complaint to the Financial Ombudsman Service, free of charge, within six months. See financial-ombudsman.org.uk. Eligibility depends on your circumstances and the nature of the matter.

4. Compensation

The fund is an unapproved Alternative Investment Fund and is a high-risk investment. As set out in our risk warning, you are unlikely to be protected if something goes wrong, and access to the Financial Services Compensation Scheme may be limited or unavailable. The Information Memorandum sets out the position in full.

5. Contact

Any questions about this procedure? Email farid@doublecheque.com.
