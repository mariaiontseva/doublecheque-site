Our strategy

How we choose what to back.

The fund backs early-stage UK companies that qualify for SEIS or EIS — a generalist book of around 12 companies (no fewer than 10), blending high-growth tech with a minimum of three established, cash-generative businesses that anchor the portfolio.

Every company clears the same double-check: more than half are existing clients of our accountancy practice, so their numbers have already been examined by an accountant, and each company must hold HMRC SEIS/EIS advance assurance before a penny moves. Double Cheque sources and runs the diligence; Sapphire Capital Partners, the fund's FCA-authorised manager, takes every final decision. Because the same team advises the fund and runs the practice that supplies much of the deal flow, this is a related-party arrangement, disclosed in full in the Information Memorandum.

How charges work

Initial and annual charges are met by the investee companies, not you, so your full subscription buys SEIS/EIS-qualifying shares and maximises your reliefs. The performance fee is shared by Sapphire and DoubleCheque and applies only to cash returned above your original investment.
