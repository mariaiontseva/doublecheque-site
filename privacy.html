Privacy Policy

Last updated 2 July 2026

This policy explains how we collect and use your personal information, and your rights under UK data protection law.

1. Who is responsible for your data

Double Cheque Ltd (company number 17167808), registered office 411 Office 1.01, Oxford Street, London W1C 2PE, is the controller of the personal data collected through this website. You can contact us about privacy at farid@doublecheque.com.

2. Information we collect

  • Investor enquiries: your name, email, phone number, investor classification, the amount you are considering, and anything you tell us in the message.
  • Document requests: the email address you give us so we can send the Information Memorandum and Key Information Document.
  • Founder submissions: your company name, your name, email, website and the details you share about your business.
  • Portfolio-company portal: account details (email and company) and the updates you submit.
  • Technical data: limited information your browser sends when you use the site.

3. How and why we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry and send you the documents you request – on the basis of your consent and our legitimate interest in dealing with enquiries.
  • To assess eligibility and administer investments and portfolio-company relationships – on the basis of our legitimate interests and, where relevant, to take steps toward a contract.
  • To meet our legal and regulatory obligations – on the basis of legal obligation.

4. Who we share it with

We share personal data only where needed: with Sapphire Capital Partners LLP (the fund's manager) and Palace Ventures Limited (our FCA principal); with trusted service providers who host the site and deliver our email; with our professional advisers; and with regulators or authorities where the law requires. We do not sell your data.

5. How long we keep it

We keep personal data only for as long as needed for the purposes above, or for as long as the law and our regulatory obligations require, after which it is securely deleted.

6. International transfers

Some of our service providers may process data outside the UK. Where they do, we take steps to ensure your data is protected by appropriate safeguards.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access your data, to have it corrected or erased, to restrict or object to its use, and to data portability. Where we rely on your consent, you can withdraw it at any time. To exercise any of these, email farid@doublecheque.com. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

8. Cookies

We use a small number of cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details.

9. Changes and contact

We may update this policy from time to time; the date at the top shows the latest version. Questions? Email farid@doublecheque.com.
